The little secret

Do you want to know more about cky?

Childhood

I use chopsticks to eat rice when I was 2 years-old.
My mother thinks that my fingers are flexible and she tell me to play piano.
Eventually, I fall in love with music and it heals my heart.♡

Instagram cover

Therefore, I start playing piano when I was 4. I started up my instagram page in 2014 and upload my piano cover on instagram to record my music life.
I start to collabrate with other ig cover singers or jamming songs with them.

Self-learning

I learn guitar by myself in a Christmas Holiday. It is a struggle to learn guitar because it just like half-giving up to play piano.
However, I enjoy singing while playing guitar and I go busking with my friends.

A.N.Y

I love playing music with my friends. ANY is stand for Angela, Nicole & Yumi. We are classmates from SDAD.
We three are vocalist. I am playing guitar while Yumi is playing percussion. We sing a mash-up song of Charmaine Fong who is a Hong Kong singer.
We win the 2nd runner-up of the SDSA singing contest.

The Polyarts

I join the Polyarts family in my year 2 university life. I am the performance secretary of The Polyarts 38th Agiato.
Perform sec usually provide music performance and invite guests to our events. It is a great oppotunity to collabrate with different musicians.
